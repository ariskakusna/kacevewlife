    Tamina Teases Reunion With Nia Jax

    The return of Nia Jax on RAW last night has opened the door for a series of intriguing possibilities in the womens division on the red brand. One person who is almost certainly going to benefit from the return is Tamina, who could be on the verge of a reunion with her former partner.

    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The return of Nia Jax on RAW last night has opened the door for a series of intriguing possibilities in the women’s division on the red brand.

    One person who is almost certainly going to benefit from the return is Tamina, who could be on the verge of a reunion with her former partner.

    While it’s not a guarantee that they’ll get back together, Tamina knew what she was doing when she hinted at it on social media last night.

    Jax was able to build up some momentum for herself upon returning last night, squashing Deonna Purrazzo and reminding the world why she’s such a threat in this division.

    Tamina had a decent showing herself over the weekend at WrestleMania 36, and while she came up short during her title match on Night 2, it was still nice to see her get something of a spotlight.

    Will these two run through every single woman on RAW? Only time will tell.
