    Early Life

     Lewis Tan, the eldest of four sons, was born on February 4, 1987, in Salford, Greater Manchester, England. His parents are Joanne Cassidy, a retired British fashion model, and Philip Tan, a renowned Chinese Singaporean martial artist, actor, and stunt coordinator. In addition to Lewis, they have two sons, Sam Tan and Evan.

    Education

     Lewis Tan, a graduate of Nanyang Business School, Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, holds a Bachelor of Business (Marketing) degree.

     Additionally, he was actively involved in extracurricular activities during his time at Yishun Junior College in Singapore, serving as the Captain of the Squash team and taking on the role of Head of Logistics for the Overseas Community Involvement Programme to Cambodia.

    Career

    In the early 2010s, Tan kickstarted his career by appearing as a guest on well-known TV series like CSI: Miami, CSI: NY, NCIS: Los Angeles, Hawaii 5-0, 24, and Rush Hour. Additionally, he landed minor supporting roles in movies like Hangover 3 and Olympus Has Fallen.

     In 2018, Tan’s acting career took off when he was cast as Gaius Chau in the third season of Into the Badlands. The show received great acclaim from critics and viewers, and Tan’s portrayal of Gaius was highly praised. 

    Building on this success, he then secured a prominent role in the Netflix series Wu Assassins, which also garnered positive reviews upon its release.

     Tan’s acting career has been flourishing in recent years. One of his notable roles was as Cole Young in the Mortal Kombat reboot, released in 2021. The movie was a huge hit at the box office, and Tan’s portrayal of Cole received high criticism.

     Currently, he is busy filming the second season of Shadow and Bone while also gearing up for his role in the highly anticipated film The Expendables 4.

     Tan, a highly proficient martial artist, frequently showcases his abilities by performing his daring stunts in movies and TV series. To achieve such expertise, he has dedicated himself to training in various martial arts disciplines such as Taekwondo, kickboxing, Muay Thai, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, boxing, MMA, Kung Fu, and Wushu.
