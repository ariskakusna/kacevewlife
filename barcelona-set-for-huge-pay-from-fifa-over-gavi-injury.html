    Barcelona Set For HUGE PAY From FIFA Over Gavi Injury

    Barcelona are expected to get paid massively over Gavi injury while playing for Spain, MySportDab reports. The player is expected to be out for almost one year after getting injury representing his national team.

    30 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Barcelona are expected to get paid massively over Gavi injury while playing for Spain, MySportDab reports.

    The player is expected to be out for almost one year after getting injury representing his national team.

    Gavi injury is now expected to see FIFA make a certain amount of money which will be received by Barcelona.

    The youngster holds a huge place in the Barcelona team, and his injury could see him become a different player.

    There has been many debate on weather the player will come back to his full self or not after this injury.

    Gavi was brought out very early in Spain match against Georgia in the first half after suffering a nasty injury.

    He was immediately brought back to Barcelona who carried out their examination and confirmed the extend of the damage.

    He is expected to take at least 8 months or more to recover from the injury which will rule him out for the season and Euro 2024.

    According to reports reaching us from Transfer News Live, they confirmed FIFA will make a payment to Barcelona for Gavi injury.

    Here Is What Was Said:

    “FIFA will have to pay Barcelona €20,548 compensation for each day that Gavi will be out injured.

    The compensation is expected to hit a region of €6 Million before the player recover to full fitness.

    Gavi and Barcelona are not pleased with the injury but FIFA payment might be the only consolation to get from the whole misery.
