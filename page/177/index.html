'Kung Fu' Director-Producer, Desilu Executive Was 92

16 Aug 2024, 00:00

Jerry Thorpe, an executive at Lucille Ball and Desi Arnaz‘s Desilu Productions and a co-creator, director and producer on the David Carradine adventure series Kung Fu, died Sept. 25 in Santa Barbara, his family announced. He was 92. During his four-decade career, the Los Angeles native also worked on David Janssen’s Harry O; Our House, starring Wilford Brimley; and the longtime Jane Wyman CBS primetime soap Falcon Crest. His father, Richard Thorpe, was a prolific director at MGM whose voluminous credits included Tarzan Escapes (1936), The Crowd Roars (1938), The Thin Man Goes Home (1944), Ivanhoe (1952) and Jailhouse Rock (1957).
