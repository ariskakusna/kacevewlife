    Two crew members of Davido’s 30 BG and Burna Boy’s team have taken to social media in a bid to reignite the longstanding beef between the music stars.

    In a post that has now expired on his Instastory channel, Morgan DMW, took a swipe at Burna Boy’s righthand man, King Manny.
