    Getting dressed in the summer should be considered an extreme sport. For many of us, it's the busiest time of the year when we're juggling vacations, (multiple) weddings, weekend rooftop gatherings, al fresco happy hours, and work. Once you factor in the sweltering heat, putting together presentable, cute summer outfits can get complicated. But adding a few of the best summer dresses into your rotation can make warm-weather dressing much easier—offering a one-and-done solution.

    Whether it's a summer wedding guest dress or an airy cotton number for everyday errands, the best dresses stand out on their own. All you need is a matching handbag and a pair of your favorite summer shoes—from trendy sneakers for casual looks to strappy sandals for dressier occasions.

    The right pieces offer an infallible outfit formula followed by fashion insiders that you can turn to throughout the summer, from breezy billowing styles to mini LBDs. Here, Marie Claire's curated edit of the best summer dresses to invest in for every occasion, plus the accessories to match.

    For Casual Occasions

    Whether heading to a brunch, shopping, or exploring your city, you need an easy outfit that's both comfortable and cool (in the literal sense). The fashion crowd has recently opted for voluminous dresses in breathable cotton, poplin, and linen to beat the summer heat. Keep the comfort maxed out with sneakers or chunky sandals.

    For Formal Occasions

    There's a reason florals resurface amongst fashion insiders every spring and summer—they best fit the feel of a new season. Blooms were seen all over during Spring 2024 runways, at 3.1 Philip Lim, Givenchy, and Stella McCartney. A flowing floral number can be extremely versatile—from rooftop parties to garden soirees to outdoor weddings. Throw on a pair of strappy-heeled sandals and grab a clutch to complete the look.

    For the Office

    Nailing down your summer work outfits isn't always simple, considering your commuting needs, office dress codes, and the heat. Shirt dresses, a favorite of the fashion set, make for the easiest, most comfortable office outfits. They're innately professional, modeled off of classic button-downs, and come in the same breezy fabrics. Pair the style with loafers and, of course, a chic work tote bag.

Play around with details like ruffles, fringe, and ruching to master the art of dopamine dressing.

    The best little black dresses are a staple, and the right styles ooze elegance, making them ideal for nighttime outings—even in summer. While LBDs are nothing new, fashion insiders have been opting for LBDs with sophisticated halter necklines. The design is timeless and omits the need for a statement necklace.
