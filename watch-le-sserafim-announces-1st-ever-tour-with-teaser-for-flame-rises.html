    Updated June 28 KST:

    LE SSERAFIM has dropped the dates for their upcoming tour “FLAME RISES”!

    After revealing the motion logo for their tour, LE SSERAFIM dropped the dates and locations for their upcoming concerts. LE SSERAFIM will kick off the tour in Seoul from August 12 to 13, then head to Nagoya, followed by Osaka, Hong Kong, Jakarta, and Bangkok.

    Check out the dates below, and stay tuned for more updates!

    Original Article: 

    LE SSERAFIM is going on tour!

    On June 28 at midnight KST, the rookie girl group officially announced that they would soon be embarking on their first-ever tour.

    The group made the announcement with a dramatic logo motion teaser for “FLAME RISES,” an anagram of “LE SSERAFIM” (which is already famously an anagram of “IM FEARLESS”).
