    Diablo 4 Season 3 Start Time & Date in US, UK and Europe

    The official start date and time for Diablo 4 Season 2 is 23 January 2024 at 10:00 PDT in the US. For those in UK/Europe, that's 17:00 UTC on 23 January 2024.

    It is important to note that the Season 3 update will actually be available to download a couple of days earlier, giving Diablo 4 players enough time to update and prepare.

    Changes in the update will also reflect on the Eternal Realm, but you'll only be able to create seasonal characters and experience the Season of the Construct content starting on 23 January 2024.
