    Early Life and Education

    April 1, 1945, marked the birth of Anne Barbara (Debbie) Harry in Miami, Florida. She was adopted by Richard and Catherine Harry, who raised her in Hawthorne, New Jersey.     Harry began her music career as a backup singer for the folk group Wind in the Willows. She later met guitarist Chris Stein, who eventually became her lover and a bandmate.
