    Ultimate Team is gearing up for the biggest event of the cycle as Team of the Season squads will soon be upon us. The culmination of the form players have shown all season long in their respective leagues, is set to be rewarded by FIFA 22.

    Fans will have the chance to vote for their favorite nominees by league and influence who makes the cut for the official XI. Follow along for a detailed breakdown of the FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS including the release date, how to vote, and more.

    FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS release date

    The process for Team of the Season is very simple, EA Sports first reveals nominees for players to browse through, then comes the voting period for fans to have their say, and finally, EA Sports tallies up the votes and releases the official TOTS XI.

    Voting for the FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS is now open and will remain available until 1st May. Then on 13th May, EA Sports will reveal the official Bundesliga TOTS XI and make the players available in packs.

    Do note that there will likely be SBC and Objectives Challenges players available as well to add more Team of the Season cards to the drop.

    How to vote for FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS

    Voting for the FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS is a very simple process, and can be done on a multitude of devices. If you're looking to cast your vote, follow along with the step-by-step process below.

  • Log on to the FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS voting website.
  • Drag the player card for the nominee into the team lineup.
  • Agree to the terms and conditions and click 'Submit Vote' and that's it.

    • Note: You can vote multiple times to try and get your favorite nominees over the line and into the official XI.

    That's all there is to know for the upcoming FIFA 22 Bundesliga TOTS and how to vote. Make sure to check back with us throughout the TOTS promotion as we'll have the official team, SBC, and Objectives Challenge player information.
