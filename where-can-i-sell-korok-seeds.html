    Where can I sell Korok seeds?

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Korok seeds can be sold to a character named Hestu in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom. Hestu can be found at various locations throughout the game and will offer to expand your inventory in exchange for Korok seeds. Here are the different locations where you can find Hestu:

    1. Location: Riverside Stable

    Hestu can be found just northeast of Riverside Stable, north of Owlan Bridge. Follow the Hylia River on its left bank from Lake Hylia, past Proxim Bridge, and head north.

    2. Location: Lindor’s Brow Cave

    After completing Hestu’s Concerns Side Adventure, Hestu will move to Lindor’s Brow Cave. It is located near Lindor’s Brow Skyview Tower. Look for enemies nearby that Hestu is scared of and defeat them to complete the side adventure.

    3. Location: Central Hyrule

    After returning to the main base in Central Hyrule, you can find Hestu in a corner just past the merchant stall by a large tree on the southeast corner of the fort. Here, you can trade in more Korok seeds to expand your inventory.

    4. Location: Korok Forest

    If Hestu is not found in any of the previous locations, he can be found in the Korok Forest. The Korok Forest can be accessed by following the main questline in Tears of the Kingdom. Hestu will be waiting for you at the foot of the Great Deku Tree in the Korok Forest.

    5. Location: Lookout Landing

    Eventually, Hestu will make his way to Lookout Landing, which is Tears of the Kingdom’s hub town. Look for him in or around Lookout Landing to sell Korok seeds and expand your inventory.

    6. Location: Great Deku Tree

    In the later parts of Tears of the Kingdom, you can find Hestu at the foot of the Great Deku Tree in the Korok Forest. This is another location where you can sell Korok seeds and expand your inventory.

    7. Location: Tarm Point

    For a limited time during a specific event in Tears of the Kingdom, Hestu can be found at Tarm Point. This is another location where you can sell Korok seeds and expand your inventory.

    8. Location: Bonyard Dunes

    During another event in Tears of the Kingdom, Hestu can be found at Bonyard Dunes. Look for him in or around Bonyard Dunes to sell Korok seeds and expand your inventory.
