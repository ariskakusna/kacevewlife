      The sky was the limit for the squeaky clean teenager-turned-sultry pop starlet. Britney released her first major motion picture, , in 2002, and by the end of 2003 she had released a further three studio albums – all to critical acclaim – and opened her first restaurant. 

      During her early years as one of pop's biggest stars, Britney dated Justin for several years, but they split in 2012 and on January 3, 2004, Britney took the world by surprise when she married childhood friend Jason Alexander in Las Vegas. The singer later changed her mind and dismissed the wedding as a night of revelry that got out of control. The couple secured an annulment after only 55 hours. That trip down the aisle wasn't the only one for the singer in 2004. In September of that year, she wed Kevin Federline, one of her tour backup dancers, in a low-key California ceremony attended only by a select group of friends and loved-ones. Their son, Sean Preston, was born on September 14th, 2005, but hopes that her second marriage would stand the test of time were dashed when she filed for divorce in November 2006, two months after the birth of their second son Jayden James. She cited "irreconcilable differences" in legal papers.

      Motherhood provided a natural career break for Britney, however her personal life hit a rocky patch in 2007. After less than 24 hours in a rehabilitation facility in Antigua in February, the troubled star shaved her head, before heading back to a different treatment centre in California. She remained there for almost a month but a spate of driving offences followed and after a less than slick comeback performance of at MTV's Video Music Awards, the pressure proved too much for Britney.
