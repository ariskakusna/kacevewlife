    What is a stump jumper, and how do you become one?

    Stump jumpers, also known as blade pans, are designed to reduce the amount of damage done to rotary mower blades by foreign objects such as roots, stumps, and rocks, which can dull or chip the blades. In order to complete difficult clearing tasks with a rotary mower, the centre section of the thick metal blades that spin underneath the mower is known as a stump jumper.
